What is Gramotsavam?

Gramotsavam is an effort to bring back the spirit of Bharat by making everyone’s life a little more playful. It emphasizes the importance of physical, mental, and social well-being through sports and fun activities.

About Gramotsavam

Gramotsavam, an initiative by Isha Foundation, is a celebration of the rural culture and its sportsmanship. It is a part of Sadhguru's vision to revive traditional sports and engage communities in a more joyful, physical, and social way.

Join us at Isha Gramotsavam 2024 to celebrate this vision, and experience a vibrant community atmosphere that promotes health, well-being, and unity. Don’t miss out on the chance to be part of this exciting movement.
