What is Gramotsavam?

Gramotsavam seeks to revive the vibrant spirit of Bharat by infusing joy and playfulness into everyday life. It highlights the significance of sports and recreational activities in fostering physical fitness, mental resilience, and social harmony.

About Gramotsavam

Gramotsavam, an initiative by Isha Foundation, is a celebration of the rural culture and its sportsmanship. It is a part of Sadhguru's vision to revive traditional sports and engage communities in a more joyful, physical, and social way.

Join us at Isha Gramotsavam 2024 to celebrate this vision, and experience a vibrant community atmosphere filled with enthusiasm and positivity.
